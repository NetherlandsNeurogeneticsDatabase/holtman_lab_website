New paper on genetic subtypes of multiple sclerosis

We are excited to share a new publication in collaboration with Karim Kreft from the University of Nottingham on the genetics of Multiple Sclerosis. In a large MS patient cohort, three distinct genetic subtypes of MS were identified. These results provide new insights into the heterogeneity of MS.
